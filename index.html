  • General Information:

    What is Gunma Gohkin Philippine Corporation? ▼

    Gunma Gohkin Philippine Corporation or also known as "GGPC" a subsidiary or branch of a larger Japanese corporation, Gunma Gohkin Group. It may be engaged in producing specialized materials, components, or products, possibly for sectors like automotive, electronics, or industrial machinery.

  • Where is GGPC located at? ▼

    GGPC is based in the Philippines and operates as part of the Gunma Gohkin Group. It is located at Lot 1 Blk. 7 Circuit St., Light Industry & Science Park of the Philippines 1, Diezmo, Cabuyao, Laguna, 4025, Philippines

  • What products or services does GGPC offer? ▼

    GGPC offers precision-manufactured parts and components for various industries, including automotive and electronics. Our services may also include diecasting, assembly and testing, depending on customer needs.

  • How does GGPC ensure the quality of its products? ▼

    GGPC follows strict quality control procedures at every stage of production. We adhere to ISO standards and utilize advanced testing and inspection equipment to ensure that our products meet the highest levels of quality and precision.

  • Can GGPC handle international shipping and logistics? ▼

    Yes, GGPC has experience in handling both domestic and international shipping. We ensure that all shipments are properly packaged and delivered on time to meet our clients' global logistics requirements.
